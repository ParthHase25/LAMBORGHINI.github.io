2022 AVENTADOR 10/10 


With a raucous 769-hp V-12 engine and an exotic exterior, the 2022 Lamborghini Aventador is the definition of a hyper car. While competitors such as the Ferrari SF90 embrace an electrified future, the Lambo's 12-cylinder mill swills fuel like a frat bro chugs a beer.


2022 HURACAN 9.5/10 


The 2022 Lamborghini Huracán draws everyone's attention, but it's the way it assaults the driver's senses that makes it special. The main attraction is the 600-plus-hp naturally aspirated V-10 that is mounted in the middle of the exotic-looking Lamborghini.



2022 URUS 08/10 


On paper, the supercar-bred, 641-hp Lamborghini Urus sounds overmatched against the 707-hp, Hellcat-powered Jeep Grand Cherokee Trackhawk. However, only one of these outrageous machines owns the crown as the quickest SUV we've ever run at Virginia International Raceway in our annual Lightning Lap event.


2021 AVENTADOR 10/10 


In a world that has become heavily reliant on technology, the 2021 Lamborghini Aventador takes a more nostalgic look at things. The large Lambo is a triumphant tribute to supercars of old, with its flamboyant facade and vociferous V-12.



2021 HURACAN 9.5/10 


No matter how affordable and badass the mid-engine Corvette is, it's still nowhere near as desirable or visceral as the 2021 Lamborghini Huracán. Although the Lambo's six-figure price tag makes it basically unobtainable to the vast majority of drivers, we can still drool over its dramatic design and drop our jaws when we learn about its impressive performance credentials.


2021 URUS 08/10 


The 2021 Urus is powered by a 4.0L twin-turbocharged and intercooled DOHC 32-valve V8 engine that outputs an amazing 641-hp and 627 lb-ft of torque. That's paired with an 8-speed automatic transmission with optional manual shifting mode if you prefer that level of control.


F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is the purpose of this site?

    This site provides you all the information that you need for repair and maintenance activities on Lamborghini vehicles, in compliance with the regulation 692/2008 and subsequent amendments.

  • For whom this site is available?

    This site is available for all Independent Operators that are interested. You must be registered to obtain access to the repair and maintenance information and to diagnosis interface.

  • What information is available on the site?

    You can find all repair and maintenance information on Service information Portal that you need to repair or service Lamborghini vehicles. The information provided is exactly the same as for authorized Lamborghini Dealers. The information includes: access to repair and maintenance information, vehicle diagnostics and programming, request contact information for specific repair and maintenance information and access to courses and training information.

  • Do i need training for reparing and maintaining lamborghini vehicle?

    Yes, Automobili Lamborghini S.p.A. recommends strongly that you follow class room training before repairing Lamborghini vehicles.

  • How can i buy spare parts and special tools for a lamborghini vehicle?

    You can buy spare parts and special tools at your nearest Lamborghini dealer.

  • Where can i contact the helpdesk for?

    You can contact the helpdesk for any questions or remarks that you may have about this site at e-mail address service.information@lamborghini.com.
    Service is available from 9.00 a.m. to 1.00 p.m. and from 2.00 p.m. to 5.00 p.m. from Monday to Friday GMT
